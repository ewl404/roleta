# **App Name**: Roleta

## Core Features:

- Dynamic Wheel Interaction: An interactive, visually fluid roulette wheel that users can manually spin or trigger via button.
- Live Segment Management: Interface to quickly add, edit, or delete wheel segments with individual color and weight control.
- Generative Suggestion Tool: An AI-powered feature that acts as a tool to reason and provide relevant wheel entry suggestions based on a user's prompt.
- Deterministic Result Logic: A precise algorithm to determine the winning segment with associated celebration animations.
- Session History: A scrollable timeline displaying the history of past winners and results within the current session.
- Theme Presets: Pre-configured visual styles inspired by premium casino aesthetics and luxury gold textures to style the wheel segments.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary: Luxurious Gold (#D4AF37) for the outer frame, center star hub, and result indicator.
- Segments: Alternating Deep Burgundy (#4A0404) and Rich Obsidian (#1A0F0D) to create a high-end, classic gaming atmosphere.
- Accent: Ivory Cream (#F5F5DC) for high-contrast segment typography and delicate rim highlights.
- Primary and Segment Font: 'Playfair Display', chosen for its elegant serif characters that reflect a premium casino aesthetic.
- Detailed metallic star icons and small circular 'light bulb' markers following the curvature of the wheel frame.
- Centralized focal layout where the ornate wheel dominates the view, surrounded by refined, semi-transparent utility panels.
- Momentum-driven spin physics featuring realistic friction-based deceleration and celebratory visual effects on result determination.
